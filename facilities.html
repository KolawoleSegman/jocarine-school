🏫 Our Facilities

Modern, well-equipped spaces designed to inspire learning, creativity, and growth.

💻 Computer Room

Modern computer laboratory equipped with up-to-date technology to introduce students to digital literacy from an early age. Children learn basic computer skills, coding fundamentals, and how to use technology as a tool for learning and creativity.

📚 School Library

A well-stocked library filled with age-appropriate books, educational materials, and resources that foster a love for reading. Our library provides a quiet, comfortable space where students can explore stories and develop literacy skills.

📖 Classrooms

Bright, spacious, and well-ventilated classrooms designed to create an optimal learning environment. Each classroom is equipped with modern teaching aids, comfortable furniture, and colorful displays that make learning engaging and enjoyable.

🏃‍♂️ Playground & Learning Spaces

Safe outdoor play areas and learning spaces where children can engage in physical activities, develop motor skills, and enjoy social interaction. Our playground equipment is age-appropriate and designed to promote healthy physical development and teamwork.
