Welcome to Jocarine International Nursery and Primary School

A child-centered school founded in 2007, dedicated to providing young children with a strong academic and moral foundation in a warm, nurturing environment.

Jocarine International Nursery and Primary School is a child-centered educational institution founded in 2007 by Mrs. Josephine Chukwujekwu, a respected retired Director of Education. We provide young children with a strong academic and moral foundation during their formative years, emphasizing early literacy, numeracy, creativity, social skills, and moral upbringing.
