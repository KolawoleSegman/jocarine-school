About Jocarine International

Nurturing young minds with knowledge, godliness, and discipline since 2007.

A Legacy of Excellence in Early Childhood Education

Jocarine International Nursery and Primary School was founded in 2007 by Mrs. Josephine Chukwujekwu, a retired Director of Education with a lifelong passion for quality teaching and character formation.

We are a child-centered institution committed to providing a strong academic and moral foundation during the most critical years of a child’s development.

Our Founder

Mrs. Josephine Chukwujekwu — A Legacy of Excellence

A Visionary in Education

Mrs. Josephine Chukwujekwu dedicated her career to raising educational standards. Her deep passion for children led her to establish Jocarine International in 2007, creating a nurturing environment where academic excellence meets strong moral upbringing.

Her philosophy continues to guide every decision we make today.

"Every child deserves a foundation that nurtures both mind and character."
— Mrs. Josephine Chukwujekwu
