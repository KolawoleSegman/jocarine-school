About Jocarine International Nursery and Primary School

Jocarine International Nursery and Primary School is a child-centered educational institution founded in 2007 by Mrs. Josephine Chukwujekwu, a respected retired Director of Education whose lifelong career was devoted to improving standards in teaching and learning. The school was established out of her passion to provide young children with a strong academic and moral foundation during their formative years.

Built on the core values of Knowledge, Godliness, and Discipline, the school is committed to nurturing well-rounded pupils through quality instruction, character development, and a supportive learning environment.

The school emphasizes early literacy, numeracy, creativity, social skills, and moral upbringing, ensuring learners grow into confident, responsible, and intellectually curious individuals.

Jocarine International Nursery and Primary School believes strongly in close collaboration between parents, teachers, and pupils, recognizing that a strong home–school partnership is essential for every child’s success.

Our Founder

Mrs. Josephine Chukwujekwu — A Legacy of Excellence

A Respected Retired Director of Education

Mrs. Josephine Chukwujekwu dedicated her lifelong career to improving standards in teaching and learning. Her passion for providing young children with strong academic and moral foundations inspired the establishment of Jocarine International in 2007, creating a legacy of excellence in early childhood education.

Under her visionary leadership, the school has grown into a beacon of quality education, blending modern pedagogy with timeless values. Her hands-on approach and commitment to each child's holistic development continue to guide the school's philosophy today.

Every child deserves a foundation that nurtures both mind and character. — Mrs. Josephine Chukwujekwu
